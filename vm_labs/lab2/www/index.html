What is Social Engineering?

Psychological Manipulation

Social engineering exploits human psychology rather than technical vulnerabilities to gain access to systems, data, or physical locations.

Common Techniques

Phishing, pretexting, baiting, quid pro quo, and tailgating are among the most prevalent social engineering tactics.

Protection Strategies

Awareness, verification, and skepticism are your best defenses. Always verify identities and be cautious with sensitive information.

Training Importance

Regular training and simulated attacks help organizations and individuals recognize and respond to social engineering attempts.
